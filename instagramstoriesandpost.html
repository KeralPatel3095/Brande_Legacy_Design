FAQ

frequently asked questions

Do you provide captions?

Yes, all posts come with engagement-ready copy.

What file types do I receive?

Typically high-resolution PNG or JPEG for feed and story frames. If you need layered source files for your team, we can include that in scope.

Can I book only stories or only feed posts?

Yes. Asset kits can be weighted toward stories, carousels, or static posts depending on what your funnel needs next.

How does this work with social media management?

When bundled, creative is planned alongside your calendar and approvals so grids and stories stay on message. Standalone kits are yours to slot in whenever you post.

How fast is turnaround?

Timelines depend on batch size and revisions; after a kickoff you’ll get a clear delivery schedule for each drop or monthly set.

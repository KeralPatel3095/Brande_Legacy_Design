frequently asked questions

How quickly can a campaign go live?

Starter campaigns are typically live within 3–5 business days of brief approval. Monthly packages begin immediately after onboarding no long runway before you see results.

How do you ensure emails look right everywhere?

Every campaign is tested across devices, screen sizes, and dark mode before it goes live. What you approve is what your audience receives no surprises.

Can you work within my existing email platform?

Yes, Mailchimp, Klaviyo, and most major platforms. We work within your current stack and improve what's there, rather than forcing a migration you don't need.

What does optimisation actually involve?

Open rate, click rate, conversion, and list health reviewed monthly. Every decision is made from what the data shows, not what we assumed when we started.

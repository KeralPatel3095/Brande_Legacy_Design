FAQ

frequently asked questions

How is social media management different from just scheduling posts?

Scheduling is the final step. Before that comes strategy: goals, audience, positioning, and content pillars so each post earns its place in the calendar and supports awareness, trust, or conversion not filler.

What is a content pillar, and why use them?

Pillars are a small set of recurring themes education, proof, culture, offers, and the like that rotate through your feed. They keep the grid varied but intentional, so followers learn what you stand for and what to expect from you.

How do you keep feed posts, stories, and reels feeling like one brand?

We work from a shared visual and verbal system: typography, color, pacing, and the Linen & Layout editorial standard. Motion and static assets are designed against that system so nothing reads as a random trend bolted onto your identity.

How do you decide what to publish and when?

We align your real-world rhythm launches, seasons, appointments, campaigns with how your audience actually behaves on each surface, then sequence topics so they build on each other instead of competing for the same moment.

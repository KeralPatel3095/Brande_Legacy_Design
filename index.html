FAQ

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Here's what most of our clients want to know before we start working together.

What services does Brandé Legacy Design provide?

We offer brand identity design, brand strategy, social media management, email marketing campaigns, and design services — all tailored for small businesses and startups who want to grow with intention.

How do you create a strategy for my brand?

Every strategy starts with a discovery session where we learn your business inside out your audience, your goals, your competitors. From there, we build a brand roadmap that's custom to you, not copied from a template.

How long does it take to launch a project?

Project timelines depend on scope, but most engagements begin with planning and setup in the first 1-2 weeks, followed by launch and ongoing optimization.

Do you provide performance reports?

Yes. We share clear performance insights, including reach, engagement, and campaign outcomes, so you can see what is working and where we optimize next.

Can I request revisions to content and designs?

Absolutely. Revisions are part of the workflow to ensure the final output reflects your brand standards and communicates your message effectively.
